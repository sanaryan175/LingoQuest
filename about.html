About LingoQuest

Empowering language learners since 2025

Our Mission

LingoQuest is dedicated to helping individuals master global languages in a fun, interactive, and structured environment. We believe that language learning can change lives — opening up opportunities for career, travel, and global connection.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced and free language instructors
  • Courses for German, Spanish, Hindi & more
  • Online + offline classes with interactive materials
  • International exam preparation (Goethe, DELE, etc.)
  • Affordable pricing and flexible schedules

Our Story

This web app was built with a vision to make language learning more accessible and engaging. Sanskriti Gupta and Jainam Dudhediya, students of Second Year at Pune Institute of Technology, contributed to this project with the goal of helping people learn multiple languages in an easy and interactive way.
