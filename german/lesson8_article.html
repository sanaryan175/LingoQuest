Lesson 8: Food and Drinks in German

Let’s explore how to talk about meals, snacks, and beverages in German!

🍽️ Basic Food Vocabulary

  • das Brot – bread
  • der Käse – cheese
  • die Suppe – soup
  • das Fleisch – meat
  • das Gemüse – vegetables
  • das Obst – fruits
  • die Kartoffel – potato
  • der Reis – rice

🥤 Common Drinks

  • das Wasser – water
  • der Saft – juice
  • die Milch – milk
  • der Kaffee – coffee
  • der Tee – tea
  • die Limonade – lemonade

💡 Grammar & Sentence Structure

  • Ich esse... – I eat...
  • Ich trinke... – I drink...
  • Ich habe Hunger – I am hungry
  • Ich habe Durst – I am thirsty
  • Der/Die/Das are articles based on gender of the noun (der Saft, die Suppe, das Wasser).

📘 Easy Example Sentences

Ich esse gerne Brot und Käse.
– I like eating bread and cheese.
(Ich = I, esse = eat, gerne = like, Brot = bread, Käse = cheese)

Ich trinke jeden Morgen Kaffee.
– I drink coffee every morning.
(Ich = I, trinke = drink, jeden Morgen = every morning, Kaffee = coffee)

Wir haben Suppe zum Mittagessen.
– We have soup for lunch.
(Wir = we, haben = have, Suppe = soup, zum Mittagessen = for lunch)

Magst du Obst und Gemüse?
– Do you like fruits and vegetables?
(Magst du = Do you like, Obst = fruits, Gemüse = vegetables)

💭 Did You Know?

  • 📚 In German, every noun starts with a capital letter!
    For example: das Brot (the bread), not das brot.
    ✅ Always write nouns with a capital letter!
  • 🧠 German has **three articles** depending on the gender of the noun:
    • der – for **masculine** nouns
      (e.g. der Käse 🧀 – the cheese)
    • die – for **feminine** nouns
      (e.g. die Suppe 🍲 – the soup)
    • das – for **neuter** nouns
      (e.g. das Wasser 💧 – the water)
    📝 Tip: Always learn the article with the word!
  • 🍽️ To politely ask for something (like food or drink), say:
    Ich hätte gern... = “I would like...”
    Example: Ich hätte gern einen Kaffee (I’d like a coffee)
  • 🥦 To say **"I’m vegetarian"** in German:
    – Male: Ich bin Vegetarier
    – Female: Ich bin Vegetarierin
  • 🗣️ Want to offer something? Use:
    Möchtest du...? = “Would you like...?”
    Example: Möchtest du Tee? (Would you like tea?)
  • 🧊 Germans often drink **sparkling water** more than still water!
    – Sparkling water = Sprudelwasser
    – Still water = stilles Wasser
    So if you want plain water, ask for stilles Wasser.
